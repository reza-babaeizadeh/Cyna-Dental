Our Dentists

Highly qualified dental professionals with years of experience

Dr. Sarah Chen

General Dentist, DDS

Education: University of Toronto, DDS 2008

Specializations: Family Dentistry, Cosmetic Procedures, Root Canal Therapy

Languages: English, Mandarin, Cantonese

Dr. Chen brings over 15 years of dental experience to Cyna Dental. She is known for her gentle approach and commitment to patient comfort. Her expertise in family dentistry makes her a favorite among patients of all ages.

"I believe every patient deserves personalized care that addresses their unique needs and concerns. Building trust through clear communication is the foundation of great dental relationships."

Dr. Michael Rodriguez

Pediatric Dentist, DDS

Education: University of Western Ontario, DDS 2010

Specializations: Pediatric Dentistry, Orthodontics, Special Needs Care

Languages: English, Spanish

Dr. Rodriguez specializes in pediatric dentistry with additional training in child psychology and behavior management. He creates a fun, positive experience that helps children develop lifelong healthy dental habits.

"Making dentistry fun and stress-free for children is my passion. When kids enjoy their dental visits, they're more likely to maintain good oral health throughout their lives."
